FILES ENCRYPTION

Once the files are saved, they will be encrypted so that they cannot be accessed by anyone. A decryption key will then be given to you so that you can share these encrypted files. Encrypted files will available during 24 hours. After this duration, those files will be deleted.

SHARE YOUR LINK

You will then be given a sharing link to share the previously encrypted files. Be careful ! This sharing link will give anyone who has it free access to your encrypted files. Be sure to keep it in a safe place and share it only with the recipients.
